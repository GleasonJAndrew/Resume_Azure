ABOUT ME

I’m Andrew Gleason — born and raised in La Crosse, Wisconsin, now happily settled in Charleston, South Carolina with my beautiful wife, our daughter, and our two dogs. When I’m not deep in virtualization or cloud architecture, you’ll usually find me outside tending to my garden, in the kitchen cooking with my wife, or at the table locked into a board game together. Great food, great company, and great strategy games are pretty much my perfect weekend.

Professionally, I’m an IT Support Specialist and Infrastructure Engineer with over 20 years of hands‑on experience supporting, designing, and optimizing enterprise‑grade systems across complex industrial environments. My background spans server administration, networking, virtualization, and security — with extensive expertise in full end‑to‑end VM lifecycle management within VMware environments. I focus on ensuring stable, secure, and high‑performing operations through system hardening, patching, upgrades, compliance initiatives, and proactive monitoring that keeps critical systems running smoothly.

I’m experienced in coordinating software updates, production deployments, and cross‑team collaboration with vendors and engineering groups to reduce downtime and maintain reliability. I’m known for maintaining detailed documentation, runbooks, and configuration baselines that support scalable, repeatable infrastructure operations.
